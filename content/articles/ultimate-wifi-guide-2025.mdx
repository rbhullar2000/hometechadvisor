## üöÄ What‚Äôs New in Wi-Fi for 2025?

Wi-Fi has advanced rapidly in the last few years. Here's a quick breakdown of the major developments:

- **Wi-Fi 6 & Wi-Fi 6E**: Offer faster speeds, better range, and improved performance when many devices are connected. Wi-Fi 6E adds support for the **6 GHz band**‚Äîa less congested space with even faster performance.
- **Mesh Wi-Fi Systems**: Instead of a single router, mesh systems use multiple units to blanket your entire home in coverage, reducing dead zones.
- **Multi-Gig Internet Support**: New routers support faster wired speeds for fiber connections or NAS setups.

---

## Beyond Speed: What to Look For

### 1. Band Support

- **Dual-Band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz)**: Common setup, decent performance
- **Tri-Band**: Adds an extra 5 GHz or 6 GHz band for better load balancing

### 2. MU-MIMO & Beamforming

- Allow routers to send data to multiple devices simultaneously and focus signals toward specific devices.

### 3. Ethernet Backhaul

- For multi-level homes or places with concrete walls, consider mesh systems that support wired backhaul.

### 4. App Controls & Security

- Look for systems with mobile apps that offer:
  - Device management
  - Guest networks
  - Threat detection and firewall tools
